ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project is dedicated to find those who either don't have a car to drive, or nobody to drive them.

However, we also aim to decrease the use of gasoline to decrease carbon emissions, to create a better world for all of us.

This site will allow you to find and set up carpools, in order to get to the places you need.
